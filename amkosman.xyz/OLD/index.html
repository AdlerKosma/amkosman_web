Composer and singer Adele Marcia is currently studying electro acoustic composition in Berlin and Stockholm. Her background consists of various engagements in music and arts, professionally starting year 2010 as an alto-singer in Tensta Gospel Choir in Stockholm. Later writing and singing in electronic music group For BDK, 2012-2016, introduced music production and electronic experimental music. Later years have involved work with multichannel settings, ambisonics, live organ and vocals, supercollider, and music for moving picture.
